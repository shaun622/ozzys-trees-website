Our story

Tree work done the way you'd want it done

Ozzy's Tree & Stump Services started with a simple idea: turn up, do good honest work, clean up properly and treat people fairly. No big-company runaround, no surprises on the invoice.

We're based in Chermside and spend our days across Brisbane's Northside, from tight inner-suburb backyards to bigger blocks out north. Because we're local, we know the trees, the storms and the council rules around here, and we're never far away when you need us.

Every job, big or small, gets the same care. We plan the safest approach, protect what's around the tree, and leave your place looking better than when we arrived. That's the standard we hang our name on.

The right gear for the job
Our gear

Kitted out to handle the tricky stuff

Good gear means safer work, faster jobs and access to spots other crews can't reach. Here's what we bring to site.

  • Elevated work platform

    Our cherry picker lets us prune and dismantle safely up high without risking trees or roofs below.

  • Mini track loader

    A compact loader that fits through gates to shift logs and clear debris, saving your lawn and your back.

  • Tracked stump grinder

    Grinds stumps out below ground level and gets into tight backyards most grinders can't.

  • Wood chipper & tipper

    Branches chipped on site and carted away in one hit, so the mess is gone before we leave.
